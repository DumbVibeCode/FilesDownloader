Privacy Policy — Files Downloader

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Summary

Files Downloader does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything the extension does happens locally in your browser.

What the extension does

  • Scans the current web page for links to files (MP3, MP4, PDF, etc.)
  • Downloads selected files using the browser's built-in download manager
  • Saves your preferences (file extensions, parallel download count) locally in browser storage

Data we collect

None. The extension does not collect any data about you, your browsing activity, or the files you download.

Data storage

The only data stored is your personal settings (preferred file extensions and download count). This data is saved locally in chrome.storage.local and never leaves your device.

Third-party services

The extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising.
